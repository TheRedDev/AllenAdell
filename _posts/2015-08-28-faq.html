Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How is Allen Adell different from other recruiting and search firms?
A: Allen Adell knows healthcare. That is all we do. We are a top-tier retained provider offering superior results. We thoroughly interview every potential candidate, provide complete written assessments and discuss with you their strengths, weaknesses, hot buttons, and motivation for change. We are industry experts. We know the market and how to aggressively seek and engage the best talent in the healthcare industry. Since 2003, we have filled 100% of our searches. Customer satisfaction is paramount and our clients are elated with the value we provide.
Q: If Allen Adell is a premium provider, why have I not heard of them?
A: We work with a limited number of clients so that we can be a true strategic partner for all of their executive search needs. You may not know us, but you probably know many of our healthcare clients. We are a small agile firm whose years of experience and insider connections allow us to recruit extremely skilled professionals to leading healthcare companies.
Q: Where do you acquire candidates?
A: By having a singular focus on healthcare since 1989, Allen Adell has an extensive network spanning all segments of the provider market across North America. Most of our network has been built from referrals; people we have genuinely tried to help and their returning the favor. We have close relationships with an elite pool of passive candidates, the "A" players that are not looking. These superstars are not on the job boards and are happily employed. However, they have confidentially shared their career goals with us over the years, and we have the ability to make them aware of your opportunity as a potential advancement for their career. We also have the experience, knowledge, and skills necessary to actively recruit the top candidates from your direct competitors, assuming we do not already know them.
Q: What makes you better than other search firms?
A: By having access to the most desirable candidates and a very thorough evaluation process, we can greatly improve your ability to hire an "A" player, thus providing immediate ROI. Our search process has proven effective over the years, and we are outstanding at our jobs, allowing you to focus on your core business.
