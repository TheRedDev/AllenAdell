Our Managing Partner

Charles A. Haines is the Managing Partner of Allen Adell. Prior to founding his own company in 2003, Mr. Haines was a Vice President with Management Recruiters International (MRI) for over 14 years. Since staring his career as an executive recruiter in 1989, he has consulted with hundreds of healthcare organizations to define, recruit and successfully complete searches for C-level, VP and Director roles as well as for critical functional positions in operations, sales, marketing and human resources.

His driving business philosophy is:

  Opportunities never cease, only the desire to pursue them.

About Our Logo

Allen Adell's logo is symbolic of its ability to help clients look beyond conventional limits of building teams as well as guidance to individuals through the steps in their careers. It acknowledges the enhanced rewards for those few who are willing to take risks, to lead and reach the very top of their profession; those that expect excellence.
